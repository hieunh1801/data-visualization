A unique paradise with every amenity, Euybia Island was created with the best modern engineering and environmental technology.

Accommodations - The resort has over 950 guest rooms, thirty executive suites and four presidential suites.

Beaches - White sand beaches line the western shores, with excellent scuba diving and snorkeling areas to the east.

Dining – There are six restaurants at the resort: three five-star restaurants (each with a European-trained chef-de-cuisine); one casual restaurant, and two fine dining restaurants which are only accessible by boat. There are also three cafés and several snack kiosks.

Spa – Indulge in personalized treatment for the ultimate in health and beauty!

Marina – day rentals, tours, parasailing, jet skiing, glass-bottomed boat tours, catamarans, kayaks, paddle tours, sunset cruises

Activities – sailing, diving, swimming, and aerial tours of the island are available

Epimetheus Casino – spend your evenings in a unique, high quality gaming experience in the world. Visit the gold-plated statue of Epimetheus, the Titan of luck in gambling, who stands over the casino floor in all his gilded glory.

The Kronos Poker Championship August 26-28

Over 400 participants will gather for the sixth annual Kronos Poker Championships, being held in the Epimetheus Casino at the beautiful Euybia Island Resort and Conference Center.
Register today for your chance to play poker alongside world-renowned players from as far away as Tethys! Compete for your share of over one million Kronos dollars!
Doors open at noon on Friday, August 28, and play begins at 2 pm. See you there!

Thrasos 2016

Kronos is proud to host the International Thrasos Conference 2016, which includes the Caerus Awards for the best cyber-craftsmanship. The event is held on a manmade island housing the Euybia Island Resort and Conference Center.

The event takes place over three sun-drenched, fun-filled days.

Friday, August 26
  • Registration
  • Poster Sessions
  • Panels
  • Workshops
  • Sponsor Exhibits
  • Networking
  • Reception
  • Keynote Speaker Sven Alzada

Saturday, August 27

  • Poster Sessions
  • Panels
  • Workshops
  • Sponsor Exhibits
  • Networking
  • Distinguished Address

Sunday, August 28
  • Poster Sessions
  • Panels
  • Workshops
  • Sponsor Exhibits
  • Networking
  • Caerus Awards workshop (all day event). The Caerus Award is given for the best cyber-craftsmanship. Participants must register for the workshop prior to the Conference by hacking a specific website and solving a series of cyber puzzles. At the workshop, participants will be given a series of cyber, cryptographic and steganographic challenges to solve. The winner – if there is a winner – is the first person or team to solve the final puzzle.
  • Awards ceremony, or consolation party

The conference will sponsor the Vigenère Café in the Zuse Conference Room, a coffee and snack area uniquely suited for networking and collaboration.
